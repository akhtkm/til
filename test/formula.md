# Schrödinger equation

## Time dependent

$$
i\hbar\frac{d}{dt}\ket{\psi(t)}=\hat{H}\ket{\psi(t)}
$$

$$
i \hbar \frac{\partial \psi}{\partial t} = H \psi(x,t)
$$

# Discrete Fourier transform

$$
F(t)=\displaystyle\sum\_{x=0}^{N-1}f(x)\exp(-i\frac{2\pi tx}{N})
$$
